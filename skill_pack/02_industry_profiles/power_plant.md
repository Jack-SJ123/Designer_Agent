# Industry Profile: Power Plant

## Typical Electrical Deliverables
- Auxiliary distribution SLDs.
- Switchgear and transformer connection diagrams.
- Station service and load distribution drafts.

## Common Design Considerations
- Reliability and selectivity context.
- Clear source-transfer logic representation.
- Strong document consistency across sheets.

## Drafting Bias
- Keep source paths and tie-breaker states clear.
- Use unambiguous labeling for normal/backup feeds.
