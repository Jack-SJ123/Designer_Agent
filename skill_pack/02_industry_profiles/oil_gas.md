# Industry Profile: Oil & Gas

## Typical Electrical Deliverables
- Area-based SLDs and distribution overviews.
- Hazardous area equipment tagging references.
- UPS/emergency distribution diagrams.

## Common Design Considerations
- Hazardous area documentation discipline.
- Redundancy and emergency power paths.
- Strict documentation control and revision rigor.

## Drafting Bias
- Explicitly show normal/emergency source paths.
- Emphasize revision annotations and traceability.
