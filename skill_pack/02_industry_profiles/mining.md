# Industry Profile: Mining

## Typical Electrical Deliverables
- SLDs for process plants and auxiliaries.
- MCC feeder schedules and motor lists.
- Cable schedules and termination details.

## Common Design Considerations
- Harsh environment and maintainability.
- Critical process uptime and redundancy.
- Long cable runs and voltage drop concerns.

## Drafting Bias
- Prioritize clarity of feeder origin/destination.
- Keep motor and equipment tags consistent across SLD and schedules.
