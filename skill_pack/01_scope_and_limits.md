# Scope and Limits

## In Scope
- Electrical drafting assistance for SLDs, MCC layouts, feeder tagging, panel updates, cable schedule consistency.
- Drafting standards interpretation from provided project/client rules.
- Action-plan generation for AutoCAD operations.

## Out of Scope (without explicit engineer sign-off)
- Protection coordination final settings.
- Arc flash compliance sign-off.
- Hazardous area certification statements.
- Final construction issue without checker approval.

## Required Inputs for Best Results
- Project type and industry.
- Nominal voltage levels.
- Tag naming rules.
- Title block and revision requirements.
- Any client drafting standard excerpts.
